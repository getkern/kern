# kern brand assets

Official logo and marks for kern. The code is [Apache-2.0](../../LICENSE), but **"kern" is a
trademark**: please do not use these marks for a fork, a modified build, or a competing product or
service without permission. See [TRADEMARK.md](../../TRADEMARK.md).

## Files
- `kern-logo.png` . stencil KERN wordmark, transparent background (works on light or dark)
- `kern-logo-dark.png` . stencil KERN wordmark on dark (`#0d1117`)
- `kern-banner.png` . 1500x500 header (X / social banner)
- `og-image.png` . 1200x630 social card (the getkern.dev Open Graph / Twitter preview image)

## Palette
- background: `#0d1117`
- teal (the mark): `#0b7285` on light, `#39d0e0` on dark
- green (accent): `#3fb950` on dark, `#1a7f37` on light
- dim text: `#8b949e`

The wordmark is a bold monospace stencil; keep the terminal aesthetic and the teal + green pairing.
